Meet Healio,
your health AI.

Healio analyses your uploaded medical reports, understands your health history, and gives you clear, personalised guidance — not just raw data. Ask it anything about your health.

Healio speaks to you in plain language, suggests next steps, and flags anything that needs attention — before your doctor even calls.

✦ Healio AI
I've analysed your latest blood report. Your haemoglobin is at 11.2 g/dL — slightly below the healthy range for your profile. I'd recommend increasing iron-rich foods and checking with your doctor.
What foods should I eat?

Great question. For your profile: spinach, lentils, pumpkin seeds, and dark chocolate. Pair with Vitamin C sources to improve absorption. I've added this to your weekly goal.
Features

Health tracking
reimagined.

01 / REPORTS
AI Report Analysis

Upload any medical PDF — blood work, scans, prescriptions. Healio reads it, extracts key markers, and explains what they mean for you.

02 / SYSTEMS
Body System Tracking

Track health across Cardiovascular, Respiratory, Metabolic, Skeletal, Neurological, and Hormonal systems. See which need attention.

03 / FAMILY
Family Health Groups

Add family members, manage their reports, and get a unified view of your family's health. Care for everyone, together.

04 / GOALS
Smart Goals & Challenges

Set health goals guided by your data. Daily steps, hydration, sleep — with AI-adjusted targets based on your progress.

05 / TRENDS
Health Trends Over Time

See how your markers change across reports over months and years. Catch changes before they become problems.

06 / PRIVACY
Fully Private

Your health data is yours. End-to-end encrypted, never sold. Built on Supabase with row-level security.
